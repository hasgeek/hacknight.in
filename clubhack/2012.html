ClubHack 2012

Remember the side project/idea you always wanted to work on.
You always thought it would take some concentrated hours but never found those hours at all.

Let’s have a hack night in ClubHack 2012 and find that extra hour and motivation to code that fun thing you always wanted to.
A night where actual hackers spend time not to “break” into someone but to “make” something interesting.

…and the best part, get a chance to present the same to the audience of ClubHack

Do’s
    - Gather
    - Come with an idea
    - Make something like
        A security plugin for a CMS
        A module for Metasploit
        A script to detect attack
        An app
        A tool
        Something creative which can help your product/work
        Your imagination, no limits
    - Eat, Drink (Non-Alchoholic) & share knowledge
    - Bring your friend along who wants to stay awake the whole night and help you in coding
    - Help other hackers make something.
    - Solve problems

Dont’s
    - No cracking
    - No breaking laws
    - No booze, after all its about being awake & coding
    - No so called “penetration testing”
    - No malware creation
