TES & Microsoft Ventures Hackday

TIE Hyderabad in collaboration with Microsoft Ventures welcomes all TES attendees to a ‘hack day’ on Saturday 14th December.

TES and Microsoft Ventures present an exclusive hackday; a chance for top developers to delive into the world of mobile app development using Azure and Azure mobile services. The hack day will be held on December 14th at the Microsoft premises in GachiBowli, Hyderabad.

What to expect:

Get a chance to be introduced to Azure mobile services – a simple platform agnostic mobile backend as a service. Do you want to build mobile applications on Windows Phone/iOS or Android and do not want to be burdened with backend logistics – Azure Mobile services is for you. Learn about the APIs and build fantastic mobile applications.

You will get a 200$ credit for signing up and using Azure mobile services. We will guide you through the signing process and be there to answer any questions/queries right away. 

What will the day look like:

9 AM – 10 AM registrations

10 AM – 11 AM  - A workshop on developing sample mobile apps on Azure mobile services.

11 AM – 6 PM – Hack

6 PM  - 7 PM – Judging and winners announcement.

And that’s it. We will provide you with all the necessary food/drinks/coffee/tea to keep your spirits high. All that you’ll need to bring is a laptop and your high levels of energy !!
  • Sat, 14 Dec 9:00 AM — 7:00 PM 14 Dec 2013
  • Entry Fee: Free for TES delegates. Other participants can register for TES at the venue
