Hackathon @ Kayako

Hackathon @ Kayako is simply an open time & place to get together and hack on some code for a weekend, while we provide refreshments.

We bring together for a coding fest focused on building mobile and web apps. Developers of all experience levels can share ideas, collaborate on existing projects, work on new projects, and find out about great tools and new APIs to play with. It's a great way to spend the weekend at Kayako and share knowledge.

Hackers will also hear from Kayako team on building apps from scratch and best coding practices that we learnt by hard way. 

We encourage you to use the space for hacking on your own projects - you don't have to take part in the main hackaton. If you happen to build something cool with Kayako http://wiki.kayako.com/display/DEV/Kayako+Developer+Resources+Home

Please ping @Salaria and @Kayako for any queries regarding this hackathon.
