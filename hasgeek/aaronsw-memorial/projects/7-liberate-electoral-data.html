Liberate electoral data

There is a bunch of voter-related data (I'm mostly thinking electoral rolls, but maybe other stuff too) which is really hard to search through and requires a lot of patience. The idea here is to collate/scrape that data and put it up in more easily accessible and searchable form.

Other related ideas welcome.

References: http://ceokarnataka.kar.nic.in/ and http://164.100.80.163/FinalRollsearch/Home.aspx
