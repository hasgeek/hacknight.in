Online Legal Help

Like Aaron, many people face depression because of the lack of legal help they get regarding their cases. Also looking at the way crime has been rampant in our country and many resourceful people get off after committing grevious crimes because of their connections and by exploiting legal loopholes. I feel there is a need for a strong global online community which will give the legal power in people's hands and will allow them to document their case and also at times upload evidences publically so that they cannot be tampered.

The community will also invite motivated individuals from law community who will volunteer to give the legal details and help to their case and help them build their case in a stronger manner.

To the world fighting corruption and abuse of the legal system, a mass movement is required which means people coming together and making law stronger and implementable so that we are not required to take the law in our own hands.
