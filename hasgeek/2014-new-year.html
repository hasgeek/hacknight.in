New Year’s Eve Hacknight

Who wants to go to a boring new year's party and gyrate hips to silly music? That stuff is so 2013! If new year's resolutions work in practice like they do in theory, then what you do when the new year rolls in is what you'll be doing all year. How about inaugurating 2014 with a night of code? Join us.

This is an open event. Bring your own beer and energy drinks. We'll order food from nearby restaurants. We have pretty good internet access and enough fancy gear to geek out on for the full year. Bring your laptop and some ideas for what you want to do, or just come over to party with geeks.
  • Tue, 31 Dec 6:00 PM — 8:00 AM 1 Jan 2014
  • Entry Fee: Free
  • HasGeek Workshop (closed)

  • Krishna Reddy Layout, Domlur 1st Stage
