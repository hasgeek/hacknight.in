Bangalore Open DataCamp

Bangalore Open DataCamp 2013 HackDay

We are a Google Group of open data enthusiasts.  We will be having an Open DataCamp on March 2nd and 3rd and will be incorporating a hackathon to assist or create projects that are working with open data. This includes Government datasets from Data.Gov.In and UIDAI. As well as, data from other projects like India Water Portal, ASER, and KLP.
  • Sun, 3 Mar 3:30 PM — 11:30 PM 3 Mar 2013
  • Entry Fee: 100
  • Dharmaram Vidya Kshetram Research Centre
  • Sadduguntepalya
  • Bangalore, , IN
