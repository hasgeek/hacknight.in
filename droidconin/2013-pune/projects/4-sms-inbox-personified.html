SMS Inbox Personified

GMail has come-up with categorization of mails. Same thing we want to do for SMS. Our app will be categorizing SMS based on certain criteria. We are planning to use mBaaS and Android SDK to build this. mBaaS connectivity will enable analytics and backup of sms data, help you swiching phones without loosing data.

Me and team of 4 members will be working on this idea.
