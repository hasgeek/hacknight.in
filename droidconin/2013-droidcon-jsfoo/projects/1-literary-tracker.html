Literary Tracker

The users can set targets for themselves in terms of how much they want to read - in terms of hours per day or per week, etc. We will track and show them the statistics over time, with an option to share them through social media. We will also have some achievements to be unlocked. 

We will also suggest some other reads for th user based on what he has been reading for the last few weeks/months - depending on volume and taste.
